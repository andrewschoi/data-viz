## Data Sources

All primary data from `/relevant_data/Final Project [SHARED]*/` directories:
- Corporate Ownership & Occupancy Over Time (2004-2024, Boston neighborhoods)
- Census + Corporate Ownership (2020 census demographics)
- Aggregated Residential Sales + Census (2010 and 2020, by census tract, MAPC region)
- Boston Residential Sales Transactions (2000-2023, individual sales)
- Eviction Court Filings (2020-2024, statewide)
- Eviction Plaintiffs and Attorneys
- Eviction + Census Combined (tract-level, 2020-2023)
- Boston Condo Conversions
- Cambridge Condo Conversions
- MAPC Municipality boundaries (GeoJSON)
- Boston Neighborhoods boundaries (GeoJSON)

No external data sources were used in this analysis. Cash sale indicator was derived from `mortgage == 0` since the `cash_sale` column contained only null/zero values.
